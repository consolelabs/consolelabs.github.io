What’s a Pod Town OG Card

Pod Town OG Card is an NFT Card made exclusively for OG Minters - people who have minted directly from pod.so website.

The level of OG Card follows this ranking system, in which only Nekos minted from pod.so are count

  • 1 - Podians Card
  • 5 - Rookies Card
  • 10 - Apprentices Card
  • 25 - Poddies Card
  • 50 - Paw Gangs Card
  • 80 - Paw Masters Card
  • 200 - Paw Grand Masters Card
  • 500 - Paw Lords Card

By holding a Pod Town OG Card, you’re able to

  • Be certified as a Cyber Neko OG Minter - the early supporter in Pod Town Metaverse
  • Have early access to our future product (Pod Together, for example), giving feedback and vote for decision on how it goes
  • Receive benefit from every product that makes by Pod Town

Can I sell my OG Card

The Royalty amount (2%) for Original Minters will be tied to OG card. It means when you trade your card on secondary market (PaintSwap for example), this Royalty will be transferred to the Buyer wallet as well.

So in fact, you can, because it’s in your pocket and you own it.
