Why WAGMI?

After crafting tons of finance stuff and vibing with peeps from all walks of life, it hit us – it’s not just the young guns, but the adulting crew too, who need to level up their money smarts. Money’s a big deal, but it’s all about managing it right, not just stacking it high. Enter WAGMI – your guide to getting good with your green.

We believe:

  • Knowing more = smarter moves = chill life
  • Being money-savvy gets us through the crazy ride called life
  • Learning the finance game = more chances to thrive

We’re all about sharing the deets through cool stories and illustrations, featuring your pals, neko, and experiments/mochi.

Game Plan

We got together and thought super hard about what we want to do

  • Guide the youth in handling their cash.
  • Turn finance (usually snooze-fest stuff) into epic, easy-to-get comics.
  • Create a bomb community all about sharing money wisdom.

Crafting Stories

Finance for all

Real talk, finance isn’t just textbook stuff – it’s about tackling real-world probs. So we’re bringing you comics – the realest way to share our experiences and lessons, with our friends, a curious neko, a dreamy rabby, and Mochi an assistant.

Make it kid-friendly

Finance can be a total head-scratcher, so we’re keeping it chill and relatable – like explaining it to a 5-year-old.

Simple vibes only

We want everyone to join in, so we’re keeping our drawings simple and fun. They’re black and white like manga, so you can focus on the cool stories.

And that’s it. WAGMI is here to help you learn about money in the funniest way. Come join us on this super cool adventure, and let’s be money superheroes together! Remember, learning about money is a super cool thing to do.
